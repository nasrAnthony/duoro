Who's behind Duoro?

Hello! My name is Anthony Nasr. I'm a student pursuing my Master of Engineering in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Ottawa, with a concentration in Applied AI/ML.

Duoro is where I bring together design, technology, and strategy to help businesses build a stronger digital presence. Every project is handled with intention, from the first idea to the final details, because I believe a website should do more than exist online. It should shape how people see your brand.

With a background in engineering, software, and artificial intelligence, I approach web design with both creativity and structure. Duoro is not a traditional agency. It is my full creative and technical practice, built around helping businesses look sharper, feel more professional, and move with more confidence online.

How it all began

Built from curiosity, shaped by experience.

What began as curiosity became a passion through real projects, hands-on problem solving, and years of building experience across design, development, and digital strategy.

Today, Duoro is my creative and technical practice. It brings together everything I enjoy: clean design, thoughtful execution, and helping businesses create a digital presence they can be proud of.
